# EM Diary

A lightweight Engineering Manager's diary for tracking one-to-one notes across your teams.

## What it does

EM Diary lets you log, organise, and review notes from 1:1s with your direct reports. Notes are grouped by team and team member, and rated using a Red / Amber / Green (RAG) status so you can spot patterns at a glance.

- Log notes against a team member with a title, detail, and RAG rating
- View all notes per team, with seed example notes to get you started
- Notes persist in the browser via localStorage — no backend required
- Simple username-based login to keep data scoped per manager
